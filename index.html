Hello , my name is Divya and I am a fully qualified, experienced Humanistic Counsellor based in Chandlers Ford, Hampshire easily accessible from Andover, Basingstoke, Southampton and Winchester .

Counselling and Psychotherapy are clinically proven to help us understand our processes and offer us help to support our mental and emotional well-being.

I draw upon a wide range of therapies including Humanistic, Transactional Analysis, CBT and mindfulness to help you overcome a wide range of issues. I customise therapy in order to meet your specific needs . I believe every person is unique and we will work at your pace.

Sessions can be long term or short term. It's about you and I will ensure that you feel safe, listened to and facilitate the therapeutic space for you to achieve your goals through the process of therapy.

  • About me

    I was born and brought up in India and moved to the UK in 2005. My first career was in Academics in a reputable Government College in Rajasthan, India. I moved here with my preschool child and 6 month old baby to join my busy husband. In the process of settling, adapting and integrating within the community I played various volunteer roles in varying setting whilst raising my young family, training and developing my own support network.

    I enjoy working with people of all ethnicity and diverse backgrounds and I understand the impact of moving cultures and the challenges and joys it encompasses.

    I come with a wealth of life experiences and understand the emotions of sadness, anger, scare and joy. Therapy is a process of healing and change can happen once individuals accept that and take the first step towards a new future.

    I regards it as a privilege to work with people, joining them in their life journey and helping them to manage change effectively.
